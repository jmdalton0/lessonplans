All pilots are responsible to See and Avoid other traffic

  • when weather conditions permit
  • regardless of whether VFR or IFR

§ 91.113

Right-of-way Rules

Who gets priority?
  • Any aircraft in distress
When converging
  • balloons
  • gliders
  • airship
  • towing or refueling
Head On
  • both yield to the right
Overtaking
  • slower aircraft have right-of-way
  • but expect to be passed on the right
Landing
  • landing aircraft have right-of-way
  • can't force people off the runway
  • if two are landing, lower altitude has right-of-way
