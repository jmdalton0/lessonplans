Forces in Flight

Thrust vs Drag

  • Thrust is created by the powerplant pulling the aircraft through the air horizantally.
  • Drag is the friction between the aircraft and the air.

Lift vs Weight

  • Lift is produced as air flows across the airfoils (wings).
  • Weight is counteracted by lift.

Stability, Maneuverability and Controllability

Stability

The tendency of an aircraft to return to its original conditions after a disturbance.

Stability affects maneuverability and controllability.

Controllability

Capability of an aircraft to respond to pilot inputs.

Maneuverability

Capability of an aircraft to perform and withstand the stress of manuevers.
