Objective

  • Gain the necessary knowledge and skills to perform all required landings safely and consistently within standards
  • Gain an awareness of safety considerations and hazards when landing

Topics

  • Normal Takeoff
  • Soft Field Takeoff

Procedures

  • Verify "hold short" checklist complete
  • Adjust power and control rudder to roll onto runway

  • Full power - verify:

    • gauges green
    • RPMs
    • airspeed alive
  • Rotate: Vr
  • Stay in ground effect until chosen climb out speed (Vy or Vx)
  • At safe altitude (about 400 AGL) set cruise flaps, pitch for cruise climb
  • other traffic
  • groud vehicles
  • animals / birds
  • terrain
  • runway conditions

Conclusion

Takeoff is a Critical Phase of Flight!

  • sterile flight deck
  • airspeed is king (don't stall)
  • verify aircraft performance and engine conditions
  • watch for obstacles

Enjoy the Flight!
