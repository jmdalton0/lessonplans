Objective

The learner will aquire the necessary knowledge, skills, and awareness pertaining to:

  • principles of flight pertaining to stalls
  • inducing and recovering from a stall, including power on and power off stalls

Topics

  • Power Off Stall
  • Power On Stall

Common Errors

  • fixating on instruments
  • using ailerons to correct heading
  • failing to apply adequate rudder
  • pitching down to aggressively
  • pitching up to aggressively
