Objective

The learner will aquire the necessary knowledge, skills, and awareness pertaining to:

  • logbook entries and endorsements
  • responsibilities as a flight instructor including maintaining a current certificate

Topics

  • Logbooks

Logbooks

Each logbook entry must include:

  • date
  • aircraft identification
  • aircraft make and model
  • total flight time
  • departure and arrival locations
  • type of experience (Solo, XC, PIC, etc.)
  • conditions (night, IMC, etc.)

If a safety pilot was required for the flight, their name must also be included in the entry.
