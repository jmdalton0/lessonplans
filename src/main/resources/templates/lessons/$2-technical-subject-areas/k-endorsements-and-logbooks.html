Area of Operation II.K

Objective

  • The learner will understand the requirements for proper logbook entries and endorsements when giving flight training, flight reviews, and submitting students for evaluation.
  • The learner will develop skills to identify the necessary requirements that a student must satisfy based on their ratings and experience.
  • The learner will mitigate risk by properly logging and retaining all necessary records and properly preparing students for evaluation.

Introduction

Improper record keeping / validation of requirements can stall your training.

Logbooks

61.51

Each logbook entry must include:

  • date
  • aircraft identification
  • aircraft make and model
  • total flight time
  • departure and arrival locations
  • type of experience (Solo, XC, PIC, etc.)
  • conditions (night, IMC, etc.)

If a safety pilot was required for the flight, their name must also be included in the entry.
