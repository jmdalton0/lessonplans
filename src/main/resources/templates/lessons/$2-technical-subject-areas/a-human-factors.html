Objective

  • The learner will understand the causes of human factors and how they can affect safety of flight.
  • The learner will develop skills to recognize symptoms of and efficiently correct human factors
  • The learner will mitigate risk by understanding and prevening the negative affects of human factors.

Hypoxia

Hypoxic Hypoxia

lack of oxygen

  • choking
  • drowning
  • high altitude

Hypemic Hypoxia

lack of blood

  • severe bleeding
  • anemia
  • CO poisoning

Histotixic Hypoxia

cells cannot use oxygen

  • drugs and alcohol
  • poison

Stagnant Hypoxia

lack of blood flow

  • poor circulation
  • pulling Gs

Symptoms

  • cyanosis
  • headache, lightheadedness, dizziness
  • impaired judgement and slow reaction time
  • euphoria
  • drowsiness
  • visual impairment
  • tingling or numbness
  • unconsciousness

Risk Mitigation

  • understand time of useful consciousness
  • proper preflight (IMSAFE)
  • descend
  • oxygen systems

Hyperventilation

lack of CO2

Causes

  • increased breathing rate
  • stressful situations

Symptoms

  • visual impairment
  • headache, lightheadedness, dizziness
  • tingling
  • hot and cold sensations
  • muscle spasms

Risk Mitigation

  • reduce stress
  • talk, sing
  • breathe into a bag
