Task I. Navigation and Cross Country Flight Planning

Objective

  • Develop skills necessary to plan a flight, including route, altitude, airspace and airport considerations, and conditions like weather, day/night, etc.

  • Develop skills necessary to act safely and timely in diversion and lost scenarios.

5 C's of Lost Procedures

  • Confess - admit that you are lost and need help
  • Circle - don't get further off track
  • Climb - increase nav signals, better view of landmarks
  • Communicate - ask ATC for help
  • Conserve - consider fuel reserves
