Notes

Phases

  • Entry
    • stall plus yaw
  • Incipient
    • varying airspeed, vertical speed, radius of rotation
    • generally slower rotations
    • lose more altitude per rotation
  • Developed
    • everything is constant rate and radius
    • faster rotations
    • lose less altitude per rotation because rotation speed decreases
    • nose is spinning about a point on the ground
  • Recovery
    • always check POH for specific recovery procedures
    • PARE is NOT universal

Notes

Recovery

  • Power Idle
    • less messy / aggravated recovery
  • Ailerons Nuetral
    • aileron will not counteract rotation
    • aileron into the spin accelerates - increases speed of rotation
    • aileron away from the spin aggravates - changes axis of rotation to more of a flat spin
  • Rudder Opposite then Nuetral
    • counteract rotation
    • apply nuetral rudder when rotation stops to avoid spinning in the opposite direction
  • Elevator Down then Up
    • lower angle of attack to regain lift
    • recover smoothly to avoid overloading the airframe
