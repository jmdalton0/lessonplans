Purpose of Effective Assessment

Effective Assessment is useful for both the instructor and learner.

Instructor
  • evaluate student's level of understanding
  • recognize parts of instruction that need more emphasis
Learner
  • recognize parts of understanding and skills that need more emphasis
  • provide learner with guidance to improve
  • provide learner with opportunites to improve self-assessment

Characteristics of Effective Assessment

  • Objective
    • free of opinion or bias
    • avoid "halo error"
  • Fexible
    • adjust the tone, technique, content, depth to fit the circumstances
  • Acceptable
    • learners will more readily accept critique from evaluators they deem qualified
    • demonstrate authority, conviction, sincerity, competence
  • Comprehensive
    • cover strenghts and deficiencies
    • tailor evaluation to provide the most benefit
  • Constructive
    • learner should benefit and progress
    • stay positive
  • Organized
    • follow a logical pattern
  • Thoughtful
    • consider the learners need for self-esteem, recognition, approval
  • Specific
    • learner should have a clear understanding of strengths and deficiencies
