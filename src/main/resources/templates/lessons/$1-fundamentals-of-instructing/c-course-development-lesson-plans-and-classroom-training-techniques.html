Course Design and Planning

Levels of Planning

Course

The overall goal.

Syllabus and Lessons serve to reach this goal.

Example: PPL 141 Course

Syllabus

structured sequence of objectives that add up to the course goal

made up of building blocks

  • road map
  • arranges building blocks of learning in logical order
  • well defined objectives and standards of completion
  • flexible

Lesson Plans

Organized structure for a single instruction period.

Includes:

  • Objectives
  • Content
  • Completion Standards

Building Blocks

  • A discrete skill or knowledge item that can be practiced individually.
  • Then combined with other building blocks to achieve larger objectives.

Lesson Objectives and Preparation

Two Types of Training Objectives

Performance Based Objectives

Learner is expected to perform an exact procedure to a specific standard

Includes:

  • description
  • conditions
  • criteria

Decision Based Objectives

As training progresses, shift to scenario-based training to develop judgement and ADM
